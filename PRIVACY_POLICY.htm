PRIVACY POLICY MODEL FOR CROQUET SCOREBOARD and POSITION RECORDER

 

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Croquet Scoreboard and Position Recorder  for mobile devices that was created by Lab Data Generator. The Application allows observers to record the clip position (score) and ball positions during a game of Association Croquet. 

 

User Provided Information 

There is no need to register to use the application. The only information recorded is that the download has taken place.

There is NO NEED to provide any personal information at all 

Automatically Collected Information 

No information is collected automatically

This Application does not collect precise information about the location of your mobile device. 

 

Except for the position of croquet balls, no information is shared with third parties

 

Data Retention Policy, Managing Your Information

No personal information is collected and so none is retained

 

Children

We do not use the Application to knowingly solicit data from or market to children under the age of 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that his or her child has provided us with information unknowingly or without their consent, he or she should contact us at info@labdatagen.com.

 

Security

Information about croquet game ball positions and scores is transmitted to an SQL database hosted by Hostinger.com.   

 

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time for any reason. We will notify you of any changes to our Privacy Policy by posting the new Privacy Policy here and making this clear on the app downloading web site. You are advised to consult this Privacy Policy regularly for any changes, as continued use is deemed approval of all changes. You can check the history of this policy at this site.

  

Contact us

If you have any questions regarding privacy while using the Application, or have questions about our practices, please contact us via email at info@labdatagen.com
